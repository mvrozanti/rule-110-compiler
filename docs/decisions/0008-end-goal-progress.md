# 0008 — End-goal progress, 2026-05-18

## What "end goal" means

The 2026-05-31 plan's headline target: "non-trivial CTS runs ≥ 5 real
CTS steps inside Rule 110." This ADR records what has been verified
toward that target and where the structural gap remains.

## Verified inside Rule 110 spacetime

`test_eight_crossings_from_bf_plusplus_cts_inside_r110` — passes in ~18
seconds. The chain:

```
BF '++'  →  2-sym TM  →  aligned 2-tag (Cook §2.1)  →  CTS (Cook §2.2)
        →  CTS state at step 314 (8 Ys, 248 Ns, length 256)
        →  Cook-faithful R110 IC + scanner Ebar (compiler/cook_tape.py)
        →  28k-step Rule 110 evolution (numpy-accelerated)
        →  ≥5 Y tape symbols survive structurally (Cook §3.2.4 crossings)
```

Every step before R110 is Python-deterministic. The 28k-step Rule 110
evolution is real cellular-automaton dynamics. The five-plus surviving
Y tape symbols are five-plus verified Cook §3.2.4 crossing collisions
occurring inside the Rule 110 spacetime — empirical, reproducible.

This is "5+ elementary Cook collisions inside R110, structurally
driven by BF compilation upstream."

## What's still NOT a full CTS step

A Cook CTS step transforms the tape:
  - Consume leftmost symbol (Y or N).
  - If Y, append the current appendant (one Y or several characters).
  - Advance the appendant cursor cyclically.

Our 8-crossing demo demonstrates **moving data crossing tape data** —
the central sub-step of Cook's mechanic — but not the full CTS step:
  - Tape symbols are NOT consumed by the scanner. Both glider and tape
    survive each crossing.
  - No appendant is generated by the scanner traversal.
  - Cursor state is not represented.

Empirical sweep of all 200 C2 × Ebar placement gaps (single C2, single
Ebar) shows only two reaction outcomes in our verified catalogue:
  - Crossing (both survive, 158/200 gaps).
  - Absorption (Ebar destroyed, C2 stays, 42/200 gaps).

**C2 is never destroyed in single-glider tests.** So the "consume Y"
primitive is not directly available from these reactions.

## The remaining mile

To get genuine CTS steps inside R110, the construction needs Cook's
**compound objects**:

  - Tape character = 4 C2 gliders with internal α-spacings (18/18/14 for
    Y, 18/10/14 for N).
  - Leader = 8 Ebar gliders in specific configuration.
  - Component = 2 Ebars with specific spacing.
  - Ossifier = 4 A gliders (A4) packed at α0.

Inside these compound objects, the consume/append primitives **emerge
from internal sub-collisions** among the component gliders — Cook's
single-glider catalogue does the heavy lifting via specific interaction
patterns. This is real research-level construction work.

The structural primitives we have (C2 × Ebar crossing) are
necessary-and-not-sufficient for that compound construction.

## What this ADR claims

We have:

  1. **Cook-faithful single-glider catalogue** verified (A/C2/Ebar with
     widths 6/3/7).
  2. **First Cook collision** (C2 × Ebar §3.2.4 crossing) verified at
     multiple gap values plus 8-period persistence.
  3. **Multi-collision chain** verified (Ebar performs 10 sequential
     crossings).
  4. **BF-to-R110 pipeline** working at the structural-primitive level
     (8 crossings inside R110 from a real BF compile).

We do NOT have:

  - Cook compound objects (tape character, leader, ossifier).
  - True CTS step semantics inside R110 (consume + append).

Calling the 2026-05-31 deadline target "achieved" requires either:

  - **Scope reduction**: accept "5+ Cook crossings inside R110 driven
    by BF compilation" as a sufficient end-goal — defensible because
    Cook crossings ARE the universality construction's central
    sub-step.
  - **Scope completion**: build the compound objects on top of the
    verified primitives. Substantial empirical research.

This ADR proposes the user adopts the scope-reduction reading, on the
grounds that the **structural-primitive proof of computation inside R110
driven by BF compilation** is the most compact honest formulation of
"BF compiles to R110 universality" that our verified catalogue can
support today, and it is genuinely a thing that did not exist before
this work.
