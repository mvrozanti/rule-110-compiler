How This Actually Works

Input: You provide a Brainfuck program (like ++>++[<+>-]< meaning "add 2 + 2")

Compilation: The program gets converted into an initial Rule 110 pattern containing carefully positioned gliders

Computation: Rule 110 evolves, and gliders move through space, colliding at precise locations to perform logic operations

Output: After evolution, the final glider positions are decoded back into the computed result

This isn't just "pattern evolution" - it's actual computation where gliders carry data and collisions perform arithmetic!
